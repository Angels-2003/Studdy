In the Internshala Annual Ranking for student Internship selections in India, Rishi MS has been the Telangana topper and South Zone 2nd topper for three consecutive years, including 2022.

  • All India Rank: 5, 4, 5

Thank you, Internshala Team, for creating these moments and Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge for introducing Internshala to Rishi MS, which is AICTE recommended.
